What is RailGuard?

Smart CCTV for Rails

RailGuard service utilizes existing CCTV networks at railway stations for various purposes including crowd management, crime prevention, and work monitoring.

RailGuard also focuses on preventing accidental pushing incidents and suicidal activities, while providing monitoring data for flexible allocation of available services.
